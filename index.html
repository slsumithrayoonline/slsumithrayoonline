About us


Mrs. Joan De Mel
Sri Lanka Sumithrayo is a government approved charity founded in 1974, by late Mrs. Joan De Mel and was incorporated by Act of Parliament No.10 of 1986. We are part of Befriender's Worldwide and function as a non-political, non-religious organization focused on providing emotional support to help those at risk of suicide.

If you feel emotionally overwhelmed please know we are here for you. Give us a call, visit any of our centres or drop us an email. It is never too late to reach out.
